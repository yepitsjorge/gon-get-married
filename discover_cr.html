This is San Ramon, Costa Rica

The resort we'll all be staying at is called Villa Blanca and it's located just above San Ramon at the top of the Los Angeles Private Cloud Forest Reserve. Please check out the items below for more detailed information.

Welcome to Villa Blanca!

A place that's (many times literally) in the clouds.

When you arrive on November 10th we'll be picking you up from San Jose airport and heading over to San Ramon. From San Ramon we'll be driving up a small road into the mountains to the top of Cloud Forest where we'll arrive at the secluded Villa Blanca. The resort was originally owned by former Costa Rican president Rodrigo Carazo Odio who converted the property into his vision for the new national parks program.

The RSVP card said only "Chicken" or "Seafood".

What's actually on the menu?

For dinner you'll have your choice of the following entrees:

  • Grilled chicken in a three cheese sauce (gorgonzola, mozzarella, Turrialba)
  • Fish of the day with shrimp in a seafood sauce
  • New addition: Steak medallions in a chimichurri sauce
Each entree comes with 2 sides:
  • Yuca al mojo: Yuca marinated in garlic, lime, and olive oil
  • Julienne style vegetables: Thinly sliced, lightly salted veggies

In case you're wondering about your accomodations:

Here are the specifics.

We've already sectioned off cabins for anyone wishing to stay at the resort. If you'd like us to reserve one for you, please contact me as soon as possible. The cabin options are 2 person or 4 person cabins available for $148/night and $182/night respectively. Prices listed are for the whole cabin not per person. Staying in the cabins onsite will ensure that you minimize travel time to and from the venue; approximate travel time to San Ramon (the nearest city) from the resort is around 30 minutes. In order to ensure there are still cabins available please send back your RSVP as soon as possible.
