Anna

As I write this incredibly sappy letter, the bride to be is sitting right in front of me... After knowing you for almost 9 years now it's safe to say that you are the older sister I never had but really always wanted. (You know, without the arguing about me stealing your clothes, shoes, candy, etc.) Without you I'd probably have that pink strand in my hair that every "angsty teen" thinks they can rock when they really really can't. From when I was too shy to even say hi to you to the moments where I've been at my worst (figuratively speaking the worst for a high school student is her psycho ex boyfriend) you've been there without fail. Jorge is an incredibly lucky man but I think I speak for everyone in this family when I say that we're even more lucky... You've become a part of us in a way that I can't even explain but you having a key to our house when Jorge doesn't even have one starts to explain that... I love you more then you will ever know & cannot wait to be the life of the party at the open bar ;)
