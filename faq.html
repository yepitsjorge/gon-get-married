I didn't read the invitation, when is your wedding?

A wonderful question illustrating the importance of reading! Our wedding is on November 11th, 2017.

How many days do I need to be in Costa Rica?

Due to the location of our wedding and the time it would take to arrive at the venue from SJO, we advise our guests to arrive by November 10th. Your return flight can be as early as Sunday, November 12th or you could choose to join the considerable number of expats living in Costa Rica. I'll leave the return part up to you!

What do I need to bring with me?

Below is a list of items we strongly recommend you bring with you:
  • Valid Passport
  • $20 in cash (to pay the exit taxes)
  • Jeans
  • Shorts
  • Skirt if you're into that (I'm not judging)
  • Light jacket if you tend to get chilly
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Fancy wedding clothes
  • Essential toiletries
  • Sunglasses
  • Sunscreen
  • Camera
If you're joining for the week-long adventure
  • All of the above
  • Bathing suit
  • Sneakers
  • Flip flops
  • In case you couldn't read the above sunscreen
  • Going out clothes
  • Plastic bags for any dirty clothes
  • Hat

Do I need to exchange dollars for the local currency? Also, what is the local currency?

The local currency is the colón. My recommendation would be to bring a credit card with no foreign currency fees. Most places readily accept credit cards but feel free to exchange some USD to Costa Rican colónes if you also prefer to have some cash on hand.
